Category 03

Heat Exchange Systems

11 Shell & Tube Heat Exchangers

Heavy-duty shell and tube heat exchangers designed for high-pressure, high-temperature industrial processes. One fluid flows through the internal tube bundle while the other flows around it inside the shell, enabling efficient thermal transfer. Ideal for power plants, oil refineries, cement plants, and chemical processing facilities. Built to TEMA standards for maximum reliability.

12 Plate Heat Exchangers

Compact, high-efficiency plate heat exchangers using a series of thin corrugated metal plates to transfer heat between two fluid streams. Offer superior thermal performance in a smaller footprint compared to shell and tube designs. Easy to disassemble, clean, and scale. Suitable for HVAC systems, food processing, pharmaceutical manufacturing, and industrial cooling loops.

13 Complete Cooling System Solutions

End-to-end cooling system design, supply, and support for industrial facilities. We assess your thermal requirements and deliver complete solutions — from heat exchanger selection to full system integration — ensuring optimal cooling performance and energy efficiency across your plant operations.
